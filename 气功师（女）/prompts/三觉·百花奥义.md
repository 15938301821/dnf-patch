大尺度三觉能量序列，包含蓄势、中心显现、峰值爆发和收尾光场，并保持镜头与人物/Cut-in 边界。

```text
female Nen Master third-awakening energy sequence, cinematic large-scale layered aura, clear buildup, central manifestation, peak burst, and ending light field, preserve the source composition, stage continuity, camera relationship, character or Cut-in boundary, and timing
```

## 源资源约束

不得默认排除人物、背景或 Cut-in。先按 manifest 区分能量层与人物层，只修改获准视觉资源。

## 阶段验收

蓄势、显现、峰值与收尾完整连续；当前 builder 未覆盖 Cut-in 时不得宣称已交付 Cut-in。
