## 职业稳定语义

## 职业通用 Prompt

```text
female Nen Master orbiting chi orb, compact readable energy sphere, stable circular orbit arc, directional trail, consistent satellite scale, preserve the source orbit radius, anchor, rotation direction, persistent loop, and copy relationship
```

## 源资源约束

先定义单球，再按源轨道、锚点、LINK/复制关系处理；不得重新居中或统一半径。

## 阶段验收

常驻循环无跳变，球体、轨迹与拖尾方向一致，并检查共享 fighter effect 的误改风险。
